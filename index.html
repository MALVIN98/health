Low Risk
Common Names:
Lisinopril, Enalapril, Ramipril
Purpose:
Treat hypertension and heart failure
Dosage:
5-40mg/day depending on condition
Diuretics
Medium Risk
Common Names:
Furosemide, Hydrochlorothiazide
Purpose:
Reduce fluid retention and blood pressure
Dosage:

Oxygen-Related Medications

Bronchodilators
Low Risk
Common Names:
Albuterol, Salbutamol, Ipratropium
Purpose:
Improve airflow in lungs
Dosage:
1-2 puffs every 4-6 hours as needed
Corticosteroids
Medium Risk
Common Names:
Prednisone, Budesonide, Fluticasone
Purpose:
Reduce lung inflammation
Dosage:
5-60mg/day depending on severity
Anticoagulants
High Risk
Common Names:
Warfarin, Rivaroxaban, Apixaban
Purpose:
Prevent blood clots in lung conditions
Dosage:
Strictly weight-dependent, requires monitoring
