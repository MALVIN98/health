ESP32-CAM Setup Instructions

  1. Connect your ESP32-CAM to your WiFi network
  2. Upload the camera streaming firmware to your device
  3. Find the IP address assigned to your ESP32-CAM
  4. Enter the stream URL in the format: http://[ESP32_IP]:81/stream
  5. Click "Connect" to verify the stream URL
  6. Click "Start Camera" to begin streaming

Note: Make sure your computer is on the same network as the ESP32-CAM device.
